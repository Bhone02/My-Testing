Dog Description 

Breed: Beagle — known for their floppy ears, expressive eyes, and keen sense of smell.
Color: Tricolor — black saddle, white legs/chest/tip of tail, and rich brown around the face and patches on the body.
Collar: Blue with a red stripe — stands out nicely against the coat.
Expression/Pose: Alert, standing with tail up (a typical Beagle stance), looking straight at the camera.
Overall Impression:Happy and playful — the open mouth and hanging tongue suggest excitement or readiness for fun.
